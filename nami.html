The third and first female member of the group. Nami is the navigatior and the first crew member that betrayed Luffy. She take all the treasures of Luffy and escape back to his hometown. It turns out that she was being threathened by Arlong, the fish-man. Arlong occupied the village since Nami's childhood, and forced her to draw the map in order to conquer the world. She does because her mother was already killed. When she finish many maps, Arlong wants her to steal treasures which transforms her into loving money.

Although she looks warm, but she is actually cold because no one can have a step in her heart especially when she was filled with hatred. But when Luffy came and promise her to defeat Arlong, Nami knows Luffy has the ability and willing to help her. She cried and started to be warm all-out. She cried again when Arlong was really defeated which means her nightmare was gone.

Although she loves money and looks like she treasures money more than anything, she showed her feelings toward other and are willing to help others even with giving up her money. This can be shown from the only tree that they had on Luffy's ship: a orange tree, signifies the love toward her mother.
